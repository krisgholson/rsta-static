We are happy to see that you are interested in the RSTA Winter Swim program. We offer a variety of programs to suit your needs, whether you are beginner who wants to become competitive, or a swimmer wanting to improve your stroke. Combined with our summer league program, RSTA Winter Swim offers a year round swimming experience.

Swimmers participating in the winter swim program will be instructed to improve on their stroke, speed and stamina. This will require them to participate in sustained swimming activities.

RSTA has additional requirement beyond the RCC rules regarding pool use.

  1. No parents are allowed on deck once the class starts
  2. No food or drink is allowed on pool deck

Directions

If you are a swimmer new to RSTA, by participating in the Winter Swim program and meeting the following requirements, you will be able to skip the new swim test requirement while registering for the summer league.

1. Attendance requirement

You have to attend at least 8 Sunday classes or 15 Tuesday/Thursday classes.

AND

2. Waiver required for Swim Test

You will need a waiver from the Head coach at the end of your enrolled session.

Swim test option

If you do not have a waiver from the Head coach, you can request a swim test at the end of your session. Successfully passing this swim test will address the second requirement.

New swimmers not meeting above the requirements at the end of their class must take the new swim test in order participate in the summer league.

Registration.

Registrations for winter sessions open November 10th.

1. Sunday Morning Program, 8-9 AM, for ages 6 to 10

Perfect for younger swimmers, this program focuses on developing proper stroke technique. In the 25 meter pool, swimmers can learn and practice the four competitive strokes. All coaches work on deck and in the water with the swimmers to improve their technique. Enrollment is limited to 42 swimmers.
