RSTA Winter Swim Program 2012 - 2013

Registration for the winter swim program will open on or about July 25. Click here to download a pdf of the program descriptions.

September 2012

  1. Sunday Night Program, 6-7 p.m., for ages 6 to 10, Reston Community Center

    Perfect for the younger swimmer, this program focuses on developing proper stroke technique. Swimmers who wish to increase their proficiency in all four competitive strokes will find this 25 meter pool an enjoyable space in which to work. All coaches work on deck and in the water with the swimmers to improve their strokes as they practice. Enrollment is limited to 42 swimmers.

    Program dates September 16 - December 16

    No swim dates October 7 and November 25

    Requirements

    • Swimmers must be at least 6 years old by the start of the session.
    • Swimmers must be able to swim 25 meters comfortably without stopping, and demonstrate the use of two different strokes.

    Note Swimmers not meeting the requirements in the first class cannot continue in this program and will not be eligible for a refund.

    Program fee $135.00

  2. Tuesday/Thursday Night Program, 7-8 p.m., for swimmers at least 9, or 8 with 4 legal strokes, Reston Community Center

    This program focuses on improving proper stroke technique and endurance. All coaches work on deck and in the water with the swimmers to improve their strokes as they practice. Enrollment is limited to 48 swimmers.

    Program dates September 18 - December 14

    No swim dates November 20 and November 22

    Requirements

    • Swimmers must be able to swim at least 50 meters comfortably without stopping, and demonstrate the use of all competitive strokes.

    Note Swimmers not meeting the requirements in the first class cannot continue in this program and will not be eligible for a refund.

    Program fee $200.00

February 2013

  1. Spring Stroke and Turn Clinic, Sundays from 9-10 a.m., for ages 6 and older, Reston Community Center

    This program is geared toward swimmers who would like to improve their stroke technique with a specific focus on starts and turns. Swimmers practice drills to improve their technique before the summer swim team season begins. Coaches work on deck and in the water with the swimmers to correct their strokes as they practice. Enrollment is limited to 42 swimmers.

    Program Dates February 10 - May 19

    No swim dates February 17, March 24, and April 14

    Requirements

    • Swimmers must be at least 6 years old by the start of the session.
    • Swimmers must be able to swim 25 meters comfortably without stopping, and demonstrate the use of two different strokes

    Note Swimmers not meeting the requirements in the first class cannot continue in this program and will not be eligible for a refund.

    Program fee $135.00
